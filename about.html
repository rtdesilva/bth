ABOUT

UNCOVER THE UNKNOWN

Beyond The Horizon is an adventurous TV series that takes viewers on thrilling explorations of the world's most hidden and uncharted locations. Hosted by a seasoned explorer, each episode delves into the mysteries of remote islands, forgotten landscapes, and untold stories of nature and culture. With breathtaking cinematography and immersive storytelling, the series brings these extraordinary places to life.

The latest season, Hidden Island, uncovers the secrets of a lost world—an untouched, forbidden island teeming with mystery, history, and untouched beauty, revealed across three breathtaking episodes. Each episode unravels a new layer of its hidden past.

Join the adventure as Beyond The Horizon: Hidden Island premieres on June 15th, exclusively on Discovery and HBO Max.
